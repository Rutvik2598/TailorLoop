Senior Backend Engineer — Payments Infrastructure
FinCore Technologies

About the Role
FinCore Technologies is building the next generation of B2B payment rails. We're looking
for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Payments Infrastructure team and help us scale
our transaction processing system to handle 100,000+ events/second reliably.

What You'll Do
- Design and build high-throughput payment processing APIs using Python (FastAPI or similar)
- Own and evolve our Kafka-based event pipeline from ingestion to settlement
- Work closely with the data team to ensure reliable, low-latency delivery of transaction
  events to downstream consumers
- Define and defend SLOs for critical payment services; participate in on-call rotation
- Write and maintain PostgreSQL schemas and query optimization for transaction history
- Mentor junior engineers and lead technical design reviews

Must-Have
- 4+ years of backend engineering experience, primarily in Python
- Strong experience with event-driven architecture (Kafka, Kinesis, or equivalent)
- PostgreSQL or another relational database at scale — query tuning, indexing, migrations
- Comfort with distributed systems trade-offs (consistency, availability, idempotency)
- Experience designing and shipping production REST APIs

Nice-to-Have
- Prior experience in fintech or payments
- Go for performance-critical services
- Redis for caching or rate limiting
- Familiarity with AWS (ECS, RDS, MSK)
- Experience building internal developer tooling

We Do Not Require
- Machine learning or data science background
- Mobile or frontend development
- Kubernetes cluster administration
